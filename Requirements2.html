Requirements

Project Background and Client

Elastacloud Limited are a Data Science Consultancy that specializes in Data Science and its practical applications in the Business world. Renewables AI is a subdivision of Elastacloud that is currently developing a specialized product for the solar industry, and more specifically for people that are directly or indirectly involved with the production of solar energy and the revenue it generates. During the project, we were in contact with Allan Mitchell, Laura Da Silva (head of of the Data Science Division) and Bianca Furtuna (Data Science).

The way the application is currently working is as follows: the client logs into his/her personal account that shows the solar plants under his/her management and he/she can visualize a personalized view of the revenue the plants are generating on a daily basis. The application is thus using Microsoft Azure Services, data from the Dark Sky API, data from the plant/s and machine learning to predict the revenue of the solar plants up to 48 hours in advance.

Project Goals

As the application is still in its early stages, there are some limitations that affect its prediction accuracy (Random Forest Algorithm is being used that only has an accuracy of up to 80%). As a result, our clients needed a new better algorithm that could be used to predict the solar energy production based on the irradiance forecast. Our team’s first task was thus to address this problem by researching and implementing an additional machine learning model that could be used to accurately predict the energy production.

Moreover, our clients underlined the need of having a form of active interaction between the software and the users. Our team’s second task was to integrate a chatbot into their platform that would help the user with gathering quick data about their plants as well as helping them navigate through the platform.

Requirement gathering

On our first meeting with our client, we got to know the company as well as their requirements for the project. We arranged the timeline of the project as well as talked about which features will be prioritized during the development. As a result, we agreed on a set of MOSCOW requirements with our client. The highest priority requirements are: to implement a chatbot, improve the accuracy of energy generated predictions, implement energy price predictions, and implement plant performance prediction.

Persona

While planning the design of our project, we created some user personas that would reflect some typical user profiles.

Persona 1
James Smith

“I want to see the exact revenue my plant is generating on a daily basis.”

James Smith is the owner of a solar panel from South Wales. Having just started his business, he wants to see how much power his plant is generating and how he can maximize his profit by selling the it at the best price.

Name James Smith
Type  Owner of a Solar Farm
Role  Client of ElastaCloud

Motivations
  • Help reduce the carbon footprint
  • The possibility to see predictions of the price of solar energy
  • See accurate prediction of how much solar energy his plant will generate.
Goals
  • Increase his profits
  • Grow his business
  • Increase the solar energy usage in South Wales
Pain Points
  • Does not know how the prices for solar energy will be in the future
  • Does not know how much solar energy the plant will generate on the future
  • Does not know which are the best time to sell solar energy
Behaviours

Persona 2
Mary Frank

“I want to maintain the growth of the profit of my solar energy company.”

Mary Frank is an asset manager of a solar energy farm with over 15 solar panels in Southampton. She has been struggling to manage the sale of the solar energy her plant is making. Now, she is looking for a way to improve the productivity of the plant.

Name Mary Frank
Type  Asset manager of a plant
Role  Client of ElastaCloud

Motivations
  • The increasing demand of the solar energy on the market
  • The fast growth of the profit in this industry
  • The possibility to add more assets under management
Goals
  • Grow her business
  • Increase the profit of her farm
  • Manage her farm effectively
Pain Points
  • Struggles to manage this many solar panels
  • Big differences between the sale of the energy generated by the solar panels
  • Does not know which are the best time to sell solar energy

User Cases

Use Case Chatbot Start
ID UC1
Brief Description The chatbot displays a blank start box
Primary Actors User
Secondary Actors Chatbot
Preconditions None
Main Flow
  1. The user opens the emulator
  2. The emulator displays a blank chat box
Postconditions None
Alternative Flows None

Use Case Chatbot question
ID UC2
Brief Description The chatbot answers the user’s question
Primary Actors User
Secondary Actors Chatbot
Preconditions The user has the emulator installed
Main Flow
  1. The user enters a question
  2. The chatbot processes the intent
  3. The chatbot gets the data from the SQL database
  4. The chatbot displays the answer for the user’s question
Postconditions None
Alternative Flows None

Use Case Chatbot clarification
ID UC3
Brief Description The chatbot asks the user for additional information in order to answer question
Primary Actors User
Secondary Actors Chatbot
Preconditions The user has the emulator installed
Main Flow
  1. The user enters a question
  2. The chatbot processes the intent
  3. The chatbot does not have enough data to search in the database
  4. The chatbot asks the user to specify the missing data
  5. The user answers with the missing data
  6. The chatbot gets the data from the database
  7. The chatbot displays the answer
Postconditions None
Alternative Flows None

Use Case Chatbot graph
ID UC4
Brief Description The chatbot displays a graph
Primary Actors User
Secondary Actors Chatbot
Preconditions None
Main Flow
  1. The user asks for a graph
  2. The chatbot processes the question
  3. The chatbot gets the data from the SQL database
  4. The chatbot generates a chart using plotly.js
  5. The chatbot displays the chart to the user
Postconditions None
Alternative Flows None
