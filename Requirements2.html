Requirements

Project Background and Client

Our CLient

Elastacloud Limited has been a long-standing Microsoft Azure partner with specialisms in data science, data engineering and cloud. Elastacloud has had countless successes with customers over the years with some of the most challenging Azure and data-related projects for customers such as Asos, Marks and Spencers, JustGiving, IOI amongst many others.

Project Background

Elastacloud has teamed up with the largest asset manager of solar farms in Europe, Quintas Energy. In this joint venture, both companies are committed to building a data science platform, called Renewables.ai, to manage the lifecycle of solar farms from engineering to trading predictions.

Renewables.ai, as a platform, will be used to predict near-real time output of solar energy farms. In this instance predictions based on combinations of model building and anomaly detection will enable the solar farm managers to respond better and build better component inventories.

In addition to understanding the output, renewables.ai will provide decision support for energy traders and brokers that want to trade out their energy on the half-hourly market by understanding the production shortfalls, energy market movements and undersupply and oversupplies against power purchase agreements.

Renewables.ai will also focus on better data strategies and ensure that data from the plant is as real-time as possible with IoT and streaming technologies being used in ingest data into Microsoft Azure.

Some key technical challenges are:

  • Working with incomplete data
  • Using the newest techniques in Deep Learning to make better predictions by enriching data from the weather

Project Goals

We have two main broad project goals. First, to build a conversational chatbot that can interact with the Renewables.ai data science platform to provide insightful responses to questions users ask it and thus enhancing the user experience (UX) of the platform. Second, to improve the prediction model used to predict the energy output of solar energy farms.

Requirement gathering

Our requirements were gathered by having several face-to-face meetings with our client where we discussed the scope of the project and negotiated a set of MoSCoW requirements. The requirements were subsequently refined and disambiguated by having a number of video calls and Slack conversations with our client.

Persona

While planning the design of our project, we created some user personas that would reflect some typical user profiles.

Persona 1
James Smith

“I want to see the exact revenue my plant is generating on a daily basis.”

James Smith is the owner of a solar panel from South Wales. Having just started his business, he wants to see how much power his plant is generating and how he can maximize his profit by selling the it at the best price.

Name James Smith
Type  Owner of a Solar Farm
Role  Client of ElastaCloud

Motivations
  • Help reduce the carbon footprint
  • The possibility to see predictions of the price of solar energy
  • See accurate prediction of how much solar energy his plant will generate.
Goals
  • Increase his profits
  • Grow his business
  • Increase the solar energy usage in South Wales
Pain Points
  • Does not know how the prices for solar energy will be in the future
  • Does not know how much solar energy the plant will generate on the future
  • Does not know which are the best time to sell solar energy
Behaviours

Persona 2
Mary Frank

“I want to maintain the growth of the profit of my solar energy company.”

Mary Frank is an asset manager of a solar energy farm with over 15 solar panels in Southampton. She has been struggling to manage the sale of the solar energy her plant is making. Now, she is looking for a way to improve the productivity of the plant.

Name Mary Frank
Type  Asset manager of a plant
Role  Client of ElastaCloud

Motivations
  • The increasing demand of the solar energy on the market
  • The fast growth of the profit in this industry
  • The possibility to add more assets under management
Goals
  • Grow her business
  • Increase the profit of her farm
  • Manage her farm effectively
Pain Points
  • Struggles to manage this many solar panels
  • Big differences between the sale of the energy generated by the solar panels
  • Does not know which are the best time to sell solar energy

User Cases

Use Case Chatbot Start
ID UC1
Brief Description The chatbot displays a blank start box
Primary Actors User
Secondary Actors Chatbot
Preconditions None
Main Flow
  1. The user opens the emulator
  2. The emulator displays a blank chat box
Postconditions None
Alternative Flows None

Use Case Chatbot question
ID UC2
Brief Description The chatbot answers the user’s question
Primary Actors User
Secondary Actors Chatbot
Preconditions The user has the emulator installed
Main Flow
  1. The user enters a question
  2. The chatbot processes the intent
  3. The chatbot gets the data from the SQL database
  4. The chatbot displays the answer for the user’s question
Postconditions None
Alternative Flows None

Use Case Chatbot clarification
ID UC3
Brief Description The chatbot asks the user for additional information in order to answer question
Primary Actors User
Secondary Actors Chatbot
Preconditions The user has the emulator installed
Main Flow
  1. The user enters a question
  2. The chatbot processes the intent
  3. The chatbot does not have enough data to search in the database
  4. The chatbot asks the user to specify the missing data
  5. The user answers with the missing data
  6. The chatbot gets the data from the database
  7. The chatbot displays the answer
Postconditions None
Alternative Flows None

Use Case Chatbot graph
ID UC4
Brief Description The chatbot displays a graph
Primary Actors User
Secondary Actors Chatbot
Preconditions None
Main Flow
  1. The user asks for a graph
  2. The chatbot processes the question
  3. The chatbot gets the data from the SQL database
  4. The chatbot generates a chart using plotly.js
  5. The chatbot displays the chart to the user
Postconditions None
Alternative Flows None
