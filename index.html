Systems Engineering HCI Assignment

A project in collaboration with Elastacloud Limited.

Overview

Overview

Our project is a platform for solar plant owners and asset managers. It aims to improve business efficiency by applying machine learning algorithms to solar plant data. The project consists of two parts. The first part is the chatbot which will answer simple questions like "What is the total production predicted for tomorrow in my portfolio?". And the second part is about displaying the solar plant data which will include past performances and future predictions.
