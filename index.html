Overview

Elastacloud Limited are a Data Science Consultancy that specializes in Data Science and its practical applications in the Business world. Renewables AI is a subdivision of Elastacloud that is currently developing a specialized product for the solar industry, and more specifically for personas that are directly or indirectly involved with the production of solar energy and the revenue it generates. RenewablesAI's cloud-based service allows users to explore data and make better business decisions with data visulation and machine learning.
